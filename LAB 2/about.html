Hiya! My name is Marta Negro.
I'm sure the first thing you want to know are the names of my two dogs.
Ikisi is the little one, adopted in Italy in 2012, and Teaspa was adopted in Thailand in 2020.
Thanks to the experience of adopting abroad, the idea of Help the dog fly was born. So, in my case, I can say that adopting a dog brought me more than happiness.
I was born in Spain, but I've lived in many places. I currently live in Scotland. When I was a kid, I wanted to be a vet; I never made it, but I knew that my love for animals would find a way into my life.
I want to help as many animals as possible, and to be honest, my biggest dream right now is to make this platform successful and make it my full-time job.
